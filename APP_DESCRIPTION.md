# 0per8r - Focus Operating System

## What is 0per8r?

**0per8r is a desktop application that locks down your computer to enforce deep focus work sessions.**

Think of it like a digital fortress for your productivity - when you start a focus session, 0per8r:

1. **Blocks ALL websites** except Google.com and sites you specifically allow
2. **Blocks ALL applications** except the ones you're working in
3. **Enters fullscreen mode** so distractions are minimized
4. **Requires a password to stop** - preventing impulse quitting

## Key Features

### 🔒 System-Wide Blocking
- **Website Blocking**: Blocks every website except Google and your allowed sites (works across all browsers)
- **App Blocking**: Kills any unauthorized apps immediately when you try to open them
- **Works system-wide**: Not just in one browser - blocks everything on your entire computer

### 🎯 Mission-Focused Design
- **Long-term Mission**: Define your overarching goal
- **Current Goal**: Set milestones you're working toward
- **Needle-Mover Tasks**: Identify today's highest-value work

### 🎵 Focus Environment
- **Ambient Soundscapes**: Rain, ocean, fire, wind, forest, cafe, cityscape sounds
- **Customizable Mix**: Adjust volume for each sound to create your perfect focus environment
- **On/Off Toggle**: Quick control to enable/disable sounds

### 📊 Progress Tracking
- **Execution Streak**: Track consecutive successful focus sessions
- **Session Timer**: Visual countdown showing time remaining
- **Completion Tracking**: Mark tasks as complete, partial, or incomplete

### 🛡️ Anti-Distraction
- **Emergency Exit Available**: Can exit if truly needed (resets streak)
- **Password Protection**: Can't just close the app - requires password to disable blocking
- **Fullscreen Lock**: Forces you to stay focused, no minimizing

## How It Works

1. **Set Your Mission & Goal**: Define what you're working toward
2. **Choose Your Needle-Mover**: Pick the most important task for today
3. **Set Duration**: Choose 25, 45, 60, 90, or 120 minutes
4. **Allow Sites/Apps**: Add any websites or apps you need for your work
5. **Begin Execution**: Click "Begin Execution" - everything else gets blocked
6. **Work Focused**: The app monitors your activity and blocks any distractions
7. **Complete Session**: Mark task completion and see your streak grow

## The Problem It Solves

Most people struggle with digital distractions:
- "I'll just check Twitter for a minute" → 2 hours later
- "Let me quickly check email" → falling down a rabbit hole
- Opening apps that aren't related to your work
- Getting distracted by notifications and other apps

**0per8r solves this by making distraction literally impossible** - you can't access blocked sites or apps, period. It's the digital equivalent of putting your phone in another room, but for your entire computer.

## What Makes It Different

- **System-wide blocking** - Works across all apps, not just browsers
- **Application blocking** - Kills unauthorized apps immediately
- **No way to bypass** - Requires admin password to stop (you can't just close it)
- **Mission-focused** - Helps you stay aligned with your goals
- **Beautiful, minimal interface** - Clean design that doesn't add distraction
